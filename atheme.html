Xtheme Development Group

Welcome to the Xtheme Development Group website. More information about Xtheme and atheme:

Atheme is back in development! Xtheme development will continue while bug fixes and some of it's new features are brought over to Atheme.

What does this mean about Xtheme IRC Services?
Xtheme has released version 7.4.2. Xtheme will continue development until a lot of bug fixes, new features and more are brought over to Atheme. The developers or maintainers of three atheme forks (Xtheme being one of the three) were chosen to lead the new development of atheme. This will be a collaborative effort.

When will all of Xtheme's bug fixes and features show up in atheme?
Xtheme's bug fixes will be introduced in Atheme's 7.2.7 release, which is coming soon. At this time, there is not an ETA on it's release, but rest assured we are working on the release.

When will all of Xtheme's features show up in atheme?
This is a bit more of a difficult question. It really depends on the feature. If you liked a certain Xtheme feature, please feel free to post a request in the atheme issue tracker on Github. One feature that will not be introduced in atheme is ChanServ:Freeze.

Should I use Xtheme or atheme?!
This depends on what features you are using that Xtheme has that atheme does not yet have. (Such as ChanServ:Suspend) If you are enjoying a feature in Xtheme on your network that has not yet been introduced to atheme, you can wait until the functionality is added to atheme to move over. (Though we do recommend eventually moving back over to atheme, as we will be working on maintaining and developing it). An announcement will be made once atheme has most of Xtheme's features.

The Xtheme Developer (siniStar) is actively involved with the new Atheme Development, but will continue to support Xtheme until Atheme catches up in development, bug fixes and new features.

If you need support, please find us on irc.IRC4Fun.net in #Xtheme --or-- on chat.Freenode.net in #Xtheme
